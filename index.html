Weekly Training Split

Monday: Chest, Back, and Lower Abs

  • Smith machine barbell bench press
  • Seated cable press-around
  • Seated cable chest flies
  • Pull-ups
  • Chest-supported T-bar rows
  • Seated cable rows
  • Leg raises

Tuesday: Biceps, Triceps, Lateral Deltoids, Traps, and Upper Abs

  • Dumbbell curls (unilateral)
  • Rope cable hammer curls
  • Bayesian curls
  • Overhead tricep rope cable extensions
  • Cable tricep pull-downs
  • Skullcrushers
  • Seated cable lateral raises
  • Shrugs (using the machine bench press)
  • Cable crunches

Wednesday: Legs

  • Hacklift squats
  • Quad extensions
  • Romanian deadlifts
  • Lying hamstring curls
  • Donkey calf raises
  • Seated calf raises

Thursday: Pull Day (Back, Biceps, Rear Deltoids) and Lower Abs

  • Pull-ups
  • Cable rows (supinated grip)
  • Chest-supported T-bar rows
  • Dumbbell curls (unilateral)
  • Rope cable hammer curls
  • Rear deltoid flies (unilateral)
  • Leg raises

Friday: Push Day (Chest, Triceps, Anterior and Lateral Deltoids) and Upper Abs

  • Smith machine incline barbell bench press
  • Seated chest cable flies
  • Overhead rope tricep extensions
  • Skullcrushers
  • Seated lateral raises
  • Dumbbell shoulder press
  • Cable crunches

Saturday: Leg Day

  • Hacklift squats
  • Quad extensions
  • Romanian deadlifts
  • Lying hamstring curls
  • Donkey calf raises
  • Seated calf raises
